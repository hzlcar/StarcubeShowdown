Starcube Showdown

Starcube Showdown is a video game I developed for my game development capstone project, primarily using Unity and C#. It features fluid movement, sword combat, unique enemies and spawning mechanics, and a sci-fi space theme!

### Instructions ###

- Open StarcubeShowdown.exe
- On the title screen, press "How to play" for detailed controls and instructions
- Press "Start" and then press "Start" again
- Play the game and try to get a high score until you lose
- Press "Restart" to play again or press "Quit to Menu" to quit
- On the title screen press "Quit" to exit the game
